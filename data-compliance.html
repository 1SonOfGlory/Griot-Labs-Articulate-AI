Data & Compliance Standard

Version 1.0 | April 26, 2026

1. Scope

This standard covers all platform data classes, including documents, photos, audio, video, transcripts, analytics, crash diagnostics, account metadata, and security logs.

2. Data Classification

  • Confidential: authentication data, coach frameworks, private learner records.
  • Sensitive: audio/video uploads, transcript content, assessment outputs.
  • Operational: telemetry, analytics events, performance metrics, crash logs.
  • Public: approved marketing content and non-identifying aggregate insights.

3. Security Controls

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest where available.
  • Role-based access controls for learner, coach, and admin segregation.
  • Session timeout, MFA, and revocation support for compromised accounts.
  • File upload controls (size, malware scanning where supported, provenance checks).
  • Centralized logging with tamper-resistant retention strategy.

4. Privacy & Regulatory Alignment

The platform is designed to align with standard principles from GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and common enterprise governance controls (data minimization, purpose limitation, and user rights handling).

5. Incident Response

  • Continuous monitoring for anomalous behavior and abuse signals.
  • Defined triage and escalation process for security incidents.
  • Breach notification workflow in line with applicable law and contract obligations.

6. Data Lifecycle

  • Collection: only required data for service delivery and security.
  • Processing: controlled by role and purpose tags.
  • Storage: retention windows with deletion and archival protocols.
  • Deletion: secure deletion requests and legal hold exceptions.

7. Audit and Governance

Regular policy review, access audits, dependency patching, and risk assessments are required for release readiness.
