Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Griot Labs collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information for Articulate AI.

1. Data We Collect

  • Identity and contact data: name, email address, account role.
  • Device and technical data: browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers, logs.
  • Usage data: page views, session duration, feature interactions, click events.
  • Content data: uploaded files (documents, images, audio, video), text notes, transcripts, practice results.
  • Diagnostics data: crash reports, performance traces, error telemetry.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve coaching simulations and reporting.
  • To personalize practice journeys and coach dashboards.
  • To secure accounts, detect abuse, and prevent fraud.
  • To maintain reliability through analytics and crash diagnostics.
  • To communicate product updates, support notices, and account alerts.

3. Legal Basis

We process personal data based on contract performance, legitimate interests, legal obligations, and consent where required.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with cloud infrastructure, analytics, security, and support providers under contractual safeguards.

5. Retention

Data is retained only for operational and legal needs. Unless stricter rules apply, default retention is 90 days for certain session-level records and longer for account records needed for service continuity.

6. Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and encryption at rest where supported.
  • Role-based access controls and least-privilege access.
  • MFA support, session timeout controls, and device/session policies.
  • Monitoring, audit logs, vulnerability management, and incident response procedures.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal data, and object or restrict processing where applicable by law.

8. Children

Articulate AI is not intended for children under 13 without verified guardian/school authorization and lawful basis.

9. Contact

For privacy requests: privacy@griotlabs.ai
